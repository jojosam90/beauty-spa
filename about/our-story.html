How It All Began

Founded on a Simple Belief — Beauty Should Never Hurt

EstheClinic was founded in 2003 by two passionate clinicians — Dr. Sophie Chen and Dr. Marcus Lim — who shared a common frustration: the most effective aesthetic treatments available at the time were also deeply uncomfortable. Laser hair removal was painful. Skin treatments were aggressive. And clients were expected to simply endure it.

Dr. Chen and Dr. Lim believed there was a better way. Their vision was to build a clinic that combined clinical efficacy with genuine comfort — investing in technologies that were both medically proven and truly pain-free. When they opened the doors to their first clinic at 72 Tras Street, Tanjong Pagar, EstheClinic became the first laser clinic in Singapore to use pain-free laser technology as its primary treatment modality.

The response was immediate. Clients who had avoided laser treatments for years — deterred by stories of pain and discomfort — discovered they could achieve the results they wanted without suffering. Word spread. The waiting list grew. And a brand was born.

Today, EstheClinic is operated under the parent company BVA Technology Pte Ltd — a dedicated aesthetic technology company that funds ongoing investment in the most advanced treatment platforms available globally. With 5 clinic locations across Singapore, a team of over 40 specialist therapists, and more than 20,000 clients served, we remain true to that founding belief: beauty should never hurt.

Our Journey

EstheClinic Through the Years

From a single Tanjong Pagar clinic to Singapore's most celebrated aesthetic brand — each chapter of our story brought new milestones, new technologies, and thousands of new smiling clients.

2003

EstheClinic is Born

Dr. Sophie Chen and Dr. Marcus Lim open the doors to EstheClinic's first clinic at 72 Tras Street, Tanjong Pagar. The clinic makes history as the first laser clinic in Singapore to offer fully pain-free laser hair removal technology — and the waiting list fills within weeks of opening.

2007

Expanding to Holland Village

Demand from clients living in the West and Central regions of Singapore leads to the opening of our second clinic at 3 Lorong Liput, Holland Village. The expanded service menu introduces comprehensive body contouring treatments alongside laser hair removal — marking EstheClinic's evolution into a full-service aesthetic clinic.

2010

First Major Beauty Award

EstheClinic receives its first major industry recognition — the prestigious Best Laser Clinic award from Her World Magazine. This milestone validates the clinical approach Dr. Chen and Dr. Lim had championed from day one: that truly pain-free, genuinely effective treatments could and should coexist. The award is the first of many to follow.

2013

East Coast Opens & Instant Youth Launches

Our third clinic opens at 424 Joo Chiat Road, serving the growing East Coast community. The same year sees the launch of our signature "Instant Youth" programme — a revolutionary multi-technology anti-aging course that combines HIFU, RF lifting, and Pico Laser resurfacing into a single, coordinated treatment plan. The programme becomes EstheClinic's bestselling offering within six months.

2016

Serangoon Garden & HIFU Technology

EstheClinic's fourth location opens in the cherished Serangoon Garden estate. The clinic introduces HIFU (High Intensity Focused Ultrasound) technology to EstheClinic's lineup — a non-surgical face and body lifting technology that had been quietly transforming the global aesthetic industry. HIFU immediately becomes one of the most requested treatments across all our clinics.

2019

10,000 Happy Clients & Supra Body

A landmark year: EstheClinic celebrates its 10,000th happy client milestone — a moment that marks not just scale but the consistent quality of outcomes that had built our reputation one client at a time. The year also sees the launch of the Supra Body programme — a comprehensive full-body transformation package combining body contouring, skin tightening, and brightening that quickly becomes our premium flagship offering.

2022

Orchard Opens — Five Awards in One Year

EstheClinic's most ambitious project to date: the Orchard clinic at 3 Killiney Road — our largest, most premium location, housing our most advanced technology lineup. In the same year, EstheClinic receives an unprecedented five major industry awards — cementing our position as Singapore's most recognised and celebrated aesthetic clinic brand.

2024

Singapore's #1 Rated Aesthetic Clinic

EstheClinic crosses the 20,000 happy clients milestone — and earns the title of Singapore's #1 rated aesthetic clinic across all major review platforms. With over 5,000 verified client reviews averaging 4.9 out of 5 stars, EstheClinic's reputation rests entirely on the genuine, transformative outcomes we deliver every single day. The founding vision of Dr. Chen and Dr. Lim lives on in every client we serve.

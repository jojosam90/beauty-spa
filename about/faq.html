Category 2

Treatment Questions

Detailed answers about what to expect from EstheClinic treatments.

  • The number of sessions varies by treatment type and individual factors. Laser hair removal typically requires 6–8 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart for 90%+ permanent reduction. Anti-aging treatments like HIFU typically require 1–2 sessions annually. Skin brightening for mild pigmentation may need 4–6 sessions; more stubborn hyperpigmentation may need 8–12. Acne treatment usually requires 8–12 sessions. Body contouring and cellulite programmes typically run 8–12 sessions. Your specialist will give you a specific, honest estimate at your consultation — we never recommend more sessions than your condition genuinely requires.

  • The vast majority of our treatments have no downtime at all. Laser hair removal, LED therapy, body contouring, HIFU, and most brightening treatments require no recovery — you can return to work, exercise, and social activities immediately. Some treatments may cause mild temporary redness or warmth (similar to mild sun exposure) for 1–4 hours post-session. Fractional Laser resurfacing treatments may cause 2–4 days of mild skin peeling and sensitivity — your specialist will advise on appropriate post-care. We will always tell you exactly what to expect and how to care for your skin after each treatment.

  • Yes — in many cases, combining treatments in a single session is both efficient and clinically beneficial. For example, laser hair removal can often be combined with a brightening infusion in the same visit. Anti-aging RF can be paired with LED therapy. Many of our signature programmes are built around intelligent treatment combinations that amplify each other's results. The specific combinations that are safe and advisable depend on your skin condition and the treatments involved — your specialist will design your programme with this in mind, maximising results within each visit without compromising safety or skin tolerance.

  • We take this seriously. If you are not seeing the results we promised at the rate we estimated, we want to know — and we will act. Our specialist will review your programme, assess your skin's response, and adjust the protocol if needed. We take progress photographs at regular intervals so that assessment is objective rather than subjective. If a particular technology is not responding as expected, we will consider switching to a more appropriate approach. Our philosophy is simple: if you don't see results, we're not done. We will work with you until we achieve an outcome you are genuinely happy with, within the scope of what your skin condition can realistically achieve.

  • Most aesthetic treatments are not recommended during pregnancy out of an abundance of caution — even where direct evidence of harm is lacking, the standard clinical approach is to avoid non-essential energy-based and invasive treatments during pregnancy and breastfeeding. This applies to laser hair removal, IPL, HIFU, RF, and most body treatments at EstheClinic. LED therapy is generally considered safe but we still recommend waiting until after delivery and the end of breastfeeding. Please inform our team if you are pregnant or planning to become pregnant — we will advise on the safest timing for your treatment programme and help you plan accordingly.

Category 3

Pricing & Bookings

Everything you need to know about costs, packages, cancellations, and our current offers.

  • Treatment pricing varies by treatment type, the area(s) being treated, and the number of sessions. Individual laser hair removal sessions start from $80 for small facial areas and range up to $350+ for full leg or full body sessions. Skin brightening sessions typically range from $150–$350 per session. HIFU face lifting starts from $500 per session. Body treatment sessions generally range from $180–$450. Multi-session packages and programmes offer significantly better value than individual session pricing. We provide full, transparent pricing at your consultation — there are no hidden fees or surprise add-ons. You will know exactly what your programme costs before committing to anything.

  • Yes — the majority of our clients take advantage of our treatment packages and programmes, which offer better value than individual session pricing. Treatment packages are available for all our services — typically bundles of 6, 8, or 12 sessions at a package rate that represents a significant saving versus session-by-session pricing. We also offer our signature programmes (Instant Youth, Supra Body, Intensive Brightening) as comprehensive packages. Package sessions are fully flexible — they can be used across any of our five clinic locations and don't expire within a reasonable period. We do not require membership fees or subscription payments.

  • We ask that clients give at least 24 hours' notice for appointment cancellations or rescheduling. This allows us to offer the appointment slot to other waiting clients. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice may incur a $30 late cancellation fee, which is charged to your account and deducted from your next session. We understand that genuine emergencies arise — if you need to cancel at short notice due to illness or an unavoidable situation, please contact us as soon as possible and we will do our best to accommodate you without penalty. Packages sessions are never forfeited for a single late cancellation.

  • Yes — we offer several ways to save. New clients receive a first-session introductory discount on most treatments — ask our team about the current offer when you book your consultation. Referring a friend who completes their first treatment entitles you to a credit on your account. We also run seasonal promotions on selected treatments and package upgrades — follow us on social media or sign up to our newsletter to be notified of current offers. Package pricing itself represents the most consistent and significant saving, typically 15–30% versus individual session rates. All active promotions are listed transparently — we do not use high-pressure sales tactics.

  • Yes — our introductory 50% off first treatment offer is a genuine discount available to new clients on selected treatments. It is not a bait-and-switch — the discounted treatment is a full, complete session using the same technology and professional standards as any regular session. The offer exists because we are confident that experiencing our treatments firsthand is the most effective way to demonstrate the quality and comfort of what we do. There are no hidden requirements to purchase a package to redeem the offer, and no pressure to commit to anything further during your first visit. The current terms and eligible treatments are always listed clearly on our promotions page and confirmed by our team when you book.

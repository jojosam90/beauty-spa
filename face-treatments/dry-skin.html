About the Treatment

True Hydration — Deep Within the Skin, Not Just on the Surface

Dry and dehydrated skin is one of the most widespread skin concerns — yet it's also one of the most commonly misunderstood. Many people with dry skin apply heavier and heavier moisturisers, only to find their skin still feels tight, dull, and parched by midday. The reason is simple: the skin's moisture deficit is deep within the dermal layers, not just on the surface — and no topical product can deliver hydration to where it's most needed.

Dry skin (low lipid content) and dehydrated skin (low water content) are distinct conditions that often co-exist. Both are characterised by flakiness, rough texture, a tight pulling sensation, fine dry lines (distinct from aging wrinkles), and a dull, lacklustre complexion. In Singapore's air-conditioned environment, chronic skin dehydration is exceptionally common — even in people who never had dry skin previously.

EstheClinic's dry skin treatments deliver moisture, active nutrients, and structural support directly into the dermal layers using four complementary technologies. Hydra Facial simultaneously cleanses, exfoliates, extracts, and infuses hyaluronic acid and antioxidants. Mesotherapy uses micro-injections of vitamin cocktails, hyaluronic acid, and growth factors directly into the mid-dermis. Oxygen Infusion delivers pure oxygen and serums under pressure for immediate plumping and radiance. PRP (Platelet Rich Plasma) uses growth factors from your own blood to stimulate the skin's repair and hydration mechanisms from within.

  • Chronic dehydration and tightness
  • Flaking and rough, uneven texture
  • Dull, grey, or lacklustre complexion
  • Fine dry lines (distinct from deep wrinkles)
  • Damaged or compromised moisture barrier
  • Air-conditioning-induced dehydration

The Process

How It Works

A structured hydration protocol that restores your skin's moisture from the inside out.

  1. 01

    Hydration Analysis

    Your consultation begins with a skin hydration analysis using a corneometer — a device that measures the water content of the epidermis and the lipid levels of the skin barrier. This gives us an objective baseline measurement of exactly how dehydrated your skin is and where the deficit is most pronounced. We also assess your skin barrier integrity, discuss your environment (air-conditioning exposure, water quality, climate), lifestyle, current skincare routine, and any systemic factors that may be contributing.

  2. 02

    Customised Moisture Protocol

    Based on your hydration analysis, we design a bespoke moisture restoration protocol — selecting the technologies and active ingredients most suited to your skin's specific deficit. A client with surface-level dehydration may respond best to Hydra Facial and Oxygen Infusion. A client with deep dermal dehydration and a compromised barrier may benefit most from Mesotherapy and PRP. We tailor the approach precisely, and adjust it as your skin responds and improves.

  3. 03

    Intensive Infusion Sessions

    Sessions typically last 45–60 minutes. A Hydra Facial session involves a 4-step treatment — cleanse, exfoliate, extract, and infuse — all performed by the same device in sequence. Mesotherapy involves a series of superfine micro-injections across the face delivering a vitamin and hyaluronic acid cocktail — there is minimal discomfort. Oxygen Infusion applies serums under pressure for immediate plumping. PRP involves a simple blood draw, centrifugation, and reapplication — a natural, self-sourced treatment. Sessions are typically scheduled monthly.

  4. 04

    Maintenance Programme

    After your initial intensive course (typically 3–6 sessions), we transition to a maintenance schedule — usually one session every 4–8 weeks — to keep your skin's hydration consistently optimised. We also design a home skincare protocol to support your in-clinic treatments, helping your skin retain moisture between sessions. Our specialists are available for follow-up consultations whenever your skin's needs change.

Your Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Dry skin (clinically, xerosis) has a genetic or constitutional component — the skin produces insufficient sebum (oil) to maintain its protective lipid layer, leading to chronic dryness. Dehydration is a different (though often overlapping) condition where the skin's water content is low — this can affect all skin types, including oily skin. Common causes include prolonged air-conditioning exposure (which strips humidity from the air), harsh skincare products that damage the skin barrier, insufficient water intake, medications, and aging. In Singapore's highly air-conditioned environment, skin dehydration is extremely prevalent.

  • The duration of results depends on the treatment type. Hydra Facial provides immediate hydration that typically lasts 2–4 weeks, after which the skin gradually returns to its baseline unless treated again. Mesotherapy delivers lasting structural hydration as the hyaluronic acid integrates into the dermal matrix — results can last 2–4 months. PRP results develop progressively and can last 4–6 months. With a consistent maintenance programme, results compound over time and the skin's overall hydration baseline improves progressively.

  • During your initial intensive phase (first 2–3 months), we typically recommend monthly sessions. For Hydra Facial and Oxygen Infusion, fortnightly sessions may be appropriate for severely dehydrated skin in the first month. Once your skin reaches a well-hydrated, healthy baseline, monthly maintenance sessions are sufficient for most clients. Your specialist will build a schedule that balances results with practicality and your lifestyle. Many clients choose to combine their hydration sessions with other face treatments for maximum benefit.

  • Constitutionally dry skin — where the sebaceous glands produce less oil by nature — cannot be permanently changed, as this is genetic. However, the skin's condition can be dramatically and sustainably improved with the right in-clinic treatments combined with a targeted home care routine. Our goal is to restore and maintain optimal hydration levels consistently — so that your skin functions well, looks healthy, and feels comfortable at all times. Many clients find that after a comprehensive treatment programme, their skin remains significantly better hydrated than before — even between sessions.

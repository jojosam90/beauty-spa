About the Treatment

Younger-Looking Skin — Naturally, Non-Surgically

As we age, collagen and elastin production in the skin slows dramatically. From our mid-20s, we lose approximately 1% of our collagen annually — and from our 30s and 40s, the visual effects become increasingly noticeable. Fine lines develop around the eyes and mouth. Skin loses its firmness and begins to sag around the jaw and cheeks. The overall texture becomes duller, less dewy, and less resilient.

EstheClinic's anti-aging treatments target these changes at their source using three complementary technologies — each stimulating the skin's own regenerative capacity in different ways.

Radiofrequency (RF) delivers controlled heat energy deep into the dermis, stimulating collagen contraction and new collagen synthesis — tightening the skin from within. Ultrasound HIFU (High Intensity Focused Ultrasound) targets the SMAS layer — the same deep facial tissue addressed in surgical facelifts — delivering a non-surgical lifting effect. Collagen Induction Therapy (CIT) uses micro-channels to trigger the skin's repair response, flooding the dermal layer with growth factors and stimulating fresh collagen formation.

Together, these technologies address fine lines, wrinkles, sagging, dullness, and loss of elasticity — delivering results that look natural, progressive, and genuinely rejuvenating.

  • Fine lines and expression lines
  • Deep wrinkles and furrows
  • Loss of skin elasticity and firmness
  • Dull, tired-looking complexion
  • Mild to moderate facial sagging
  • Uneven skin texture and rough pores

The Process

How It Works

A methodical, science-driven approach to reversing the visible signs of aging.

  1. 01

    Age Assessment & Consultation

    Your journey begins with a thorough facial assessment. Using advanced imaging technology, our specialist evaluates your skin's age-related changes — mapping wrinkle depth, areas of volume loss, skin laxity, texture concerns, and overall tone. We discuss your concerns, your aesthetic goals (natural enhancement, not dramatic transformation), your lifestyle, and your timeline. This forms the foundation of your personalised anti-aging strategy.

  2. 02

    Technology Selection

    Not every concern requires the same technology. Our specialist selects the most appropriate combination and sequence of RF, HIFU, and CIT based on your specific concerns and skin condition. Clients in their 30s with early fine lines respond differently to clients in their 50s with more established laxity. The protocol is built specifically for where your skin is today — and where you want it to be.

  3. 03

    Treatment Sessions

    Each session typically lasts 45–75 minutes depending on the technologies used. RF sessions produce a warm, deeply relaxing sensation. HIFU delivers focused energy pulses that some clients feel as brief, intense points of warmth. CIT is combined with soothing serums that are driven deeper into the skin via the micro-channels created. Sessions are typically spaced 3–6 weeks apart depending on the protocol chosen.

  4. 04

    Collagen Rebuilding Phase

    The most significant improvements from RF and HIFU develop over the 2–3 months following each treatment session, as the body's collagen synthesis response progresses. We monitor your skin at each visit, photographing and comparing results against your baseline images. Your specialist adjusts the protocol as needed — and the improvements compound with each session completed.

Your Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

  • There is no single right age — it depends on your skin's individual condition, not a number on a calendar. Many clients in their late 20s and early 30s begin with preventative collagen induction treatments before visible aging has progressed significantly. Those in their 40s and 50s typically see the most dramatic transformative results from HIFU and RF, as these technologies address more established laxity. The earlier you start, the less dramatic intervention you'll ultimately need. We welcome clients from their mid-20s through to their 70s and beyond.

  • Results vary by technology and individual. RF collagen stimulation results typically last 12–18 months, as the new collagen produced gradually integrates into the skin structure. HIFU results for lifting and tightening typically last 12–24 months. With periodic maintenance sessions — usually once or twice a year — results can be sustained and progressively improved over time. Importantly, the skin you don't treat continues to age, making maintenance sessions a sound long-term investment.

  • RF treatments are genuinely comfortable — most clients describe the sensation as a warm, soothing heat. HIFU can be felt as brief, pinpoint sensations of heat at the focal depth (deeper than the skin surface) — clients rate it as a 3–4 out of 10 on discomfort. CIT sessions may involve a mild tingling or scratching sensation, which our therapists minimise with topical preparation. Overall, most clients find the treatments very manageable, and the 45–75 minute sessions pass quickly.

  • For initial results, most clients complete a course of 3–6 sessions. For RF and CIT, sessions are typically monthly. For HIFU, a single session can produce significant results with a maintenance session 12–18 months later. Your specialist will design a realistic treatment schedule based on your goals and current skin condition, with clear milestone check-ins to review progress. A comprehensive anti-aging programme often combines all three technologies over a 3–6 month period.

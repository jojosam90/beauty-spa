About the Treatment

Fade Dark Spots and Scars — Reveal Your True Skin Tone

Pigmentation issues and scarring affect the vast majority of people at some point in their lives — and in Singapore's sun-rich climate, skin discolouration is among the most common aesthetic concerns we treat. From age spots and sun spots to the stubborn dark patches of melasma, from post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation (PIH) left by acne to ice-pick scars and boxcar scars — these concerns affect not just how your skin looks, but how you feel about it every day.

EstheClinic uses two highly complementary technologies to address a full spectrum of pigmentation and scar concerns. The Q-Switch Nd:YAG laser delivers ultra-short, high-energy pulses that shatter excess melanin in the skin without damaging surrounding tissue — effective for age spots, freckles, PIH, and superficial scar discolouration. Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) uses broad-spectrum light to target melanin and vascular components simultaneously, making it particularly effective for sun-damaged skin with mixed pigmentation and redness.

These technologies are combined in a customised treatment protocol designed specifically for your skin type, pigmentation pattern, and scar profile. Results build progressively with each session, revealing a clearer, more even, and noticeably brighter complexion.

  • Age spots and sun spots (solar lentigines)
  • Melasma and chloasma
  • Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation (PIH)
  • Freckles and ephelides
  • Acne scars (atrophic, PIH-based)
  • Uneven skin tone and dullness

The Process

How It Works

A structured, personalised approach to achieving clear, even skin.

  1. 01

    Consultation & Skin Analysis

    Your first visit involves a detailed skin consultation with our aesthetic specialist. Using advanced skin analysis technology, we map your pigmentation patterns, assess scar depth and type, and evaluate your skin tone (Fitzpatrick classification). We review your sun exposure history, skincare routine, any previous treatments, and relevant medical history including hormonal factors that may affect melasma.

  2. 02

    Customised Treatment Plan

    Based on your skin analysis, we design a bespoke treatment protocol — selecting the most appropriate combination of Q-Switch Nd:YAG and IPL, and calibrating energy levels and wavelengths to your specific concerns. Not all pigmentation responds to the same treatment, so this personalisation is critical to achieving the best possible results safely.

  3. 03

    Laser Sessions

    Each session takes 20–40 minutes. The laser handpiece is passed systematically over the treatment areas. You'll feel a series of brief, snapping sensations — often described as a rubber band against the skin. The skin may appear mildly pink or darkened spots may temporarily darken (a normal reaction indicating effective treatment) immediately after. Sessions are typically spaced 3–4 weeks apart to allow the skin to recover and respond.

  4. 04

    Skin Recovery & Care

    Post-treatment care is a crucial part of achieving optimal results. Our specialists provide a personalised aftercare protocol including medical-grade SPF, calming serums, and skin barrier support. Strict sun protection between sessions is essential — sun exposure can trigger new pigmentation and compromise results. We monitor your skin's progress at every visit and adjust the treatment plan as needed.

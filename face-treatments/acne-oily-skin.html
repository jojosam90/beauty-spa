About the Treatment

More Than Spot Treatment — A Complete Acne Solution

Acne and chronically oily skin are among the most frustrating conditions to manage — not because solutions don't exist, but because most people are using the wrong approach. Topical products treat the surface. Antibiotics address bacteria temporarily. But without targeting the root causes — overactive sebaceous glands, bacterial colonisation, clogged follicles, and inflammatory pathways — breakouts keep returning.

EstheClinic's acne and oily skin treatment protocol takes a systematic, multi-technology approach to address every dimension of the problem simultaneously.

IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) targets P. acnes bacteria and reduces the inflammatory response at the source. LED Blue Light Therapy uses 415 nm wavelength light to selectively destroy acne-causing bacteria in the follicle without any heat or irritation — safe even for the most sensitive skin. Salicylic Acid Treatments dissolve the bonds between dead skin cells that clog pores, dissolving blackheads and whiteheads from within. RF Pore Minimising uses radiofrequency energy to shrink overactive sebaceous glands and physically tighten enlarged pores for lasting improvements.

  • Active inflammatory acne (papules, pustules)
  • Blackheads and whiteheads (comedones)
  • Cystic and nodular acne
  • Enlarged and congested pores
  • Excess sebum and shine
  • Oily T-zone and uneven skin texture

The Process

How It Works

A thorough, science-led approach to achieving and maintaining clear, balanced skin.

  1. 01

    Skin Type Analysis

    Your consultation begins with a comprehensive skin analysis using advanced diagnostic tools. We evaluate sebum production levels, pore size, the distribution and severity of active acne, skin sensitivity, and any previous treatments or medications you've used. We also discuss lifestyle factors — diet, stress, hormones, and skincare habits — that may be contributing to your skin's condition. This thorough baseline is essential for designing an effective protocol.

  2. 02

    Treatment Protocol Design

    Based on your skin analysis, our specialist designs a customised treatment protocol — selecting and sequencing the most appropriate technologies for your specific acne type and oiliness profile. A client with mainly blackheads and oily skin requires a different approach from a client with active cystic acne. The protocol is flexible and evolves with your skin as it improves throughout the course.

  3. 03

    Acne Treatment Sessions

    Sessions typically last 30–60 minutes. A typical session may begin with a gentle salicylic acid cleanse to prepare the skin, followed by LED Blue Light therapy (20 minutes of relaxing, painless light exposure), IPL for inflammatory lesions where appropriate, and RF pore minimising on congested zones. The sequence and components are adjusted at each visit based on your skin's response. Sessions are typically scheduled every 2–3 weeks for active acne management.

  4. 04

    Long-Term Management Plan

    Once your initial breakout cycle is controlled (typically within 4–8 sessions), we transition to a maintenance phase. This involves less frequent sessions focused on maintaining the improvements, paired with a home skincare protocol our specialists will recommend to support your treatment results. The goal is not just clear skin today, but the tools and knowledge to maintain it long-term.

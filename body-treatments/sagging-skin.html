About the Treatment

Firmer Body, Restored Confidence — Without Surgery

Loose, sagging skin on the body is one of the most common aesthetic concerns following weight loss, post-pregnancy, or simply through natural aging. As collagen and elastin fibres in the dermis degrade over time — or are overstretched through rapid weight or body size changes — the skin loses its ability to snap back. The result is skin that sags on the arms, hangs on the abdomen, and droops on the inner thighs and buttocks.

Until recently, the only effective options for significant body skin laxity were surgical procedures — body lifts, arm lifts (brachioplasty), or tummy tucks (abdominoplasty). These carry significant risks, costs, and lengthy recovery periods. EstheClinic now offers a genuinely effective non-surgical alternative.

Our body skin tightening protocol combines three technologies. RF (Radiofrequency) delivers controlled deep heat to the dermis and subdermis, causing immediate collagen fibre contraction and triggering progressive new collagen production over 2–3 months. HIFU (High Intensity Focused Ultrasound) targets deeper tissue layers — precisely focusing energy at the SMAS depth on the body — to produce structural lifting and tightening not achievable by other non-invasive methods. Focused Ultrasound further enhances the collagen remodelling effect throughout the targeted zones.

  • Upper arms (bat wings / bingo arms)
  • Abdomen and lower belly skin
  • Inner and outer thighs
  • Buttocks and under-buttock area
  • Inner knee
  • Post-pregnancy and post-weight-loss laxity

The Process

How It Works

A structured, personalised protocol for tighter, firmer body skin — naturally and progressively.

  1. 01

    Skin Laxity Assessment

    Your first appointment is an in-depth body consultation. Our specialist assesses the degree of laxity in each area of concern — using standardised measurement tools to grade the severity (mild, moderate, severe). We discuss the contributing factors — age, weight history, pregnancy, genetics — and set realistic expectations for what can be achieved non-surgically. Baseline photographs are taken to track your progress objectively throughout the programme.

  2. 02

    Treatment Zone Planning

    We map the specific treatment zones on your body, planning the energy intensity, depth, and technology sequence for each area. Body tissues vary in thickness and sensitivity, so this zone-by-zone planning ensures each area receives the optimal protocol. For example, the abdomen may receive a higher-intensity RF protocol, while the inner arms receive more targeted HIFU at a specific depth.

  3. 03

    RF & HIFU Sessions

    Sessions typically take 45–90 minutes depending on the number of areas treated. RF is delivered first — the treatment head moves systematically across the treatment zone. The sensation is a deep, penetrating warmth that most clients find genuinely comfortable and even relaxing. HIFU is then applied to areas requiring deeper structural lifting — focused energy pulses are delivered in lines across the tissue. Sessions are spaced 3–6 weeks apart to allow the collagen-building process to develop between visits.

  4. 04

    Progressive Firming Phase

    After each session, the collagen synthesis process continues for up to 12 weeks. We schedule progress reviews at 4 weeks and 3 months after each treatment to assess and photograph your improvements. As results develop progressively, we adjust the protocol — shifting focus to areas that need more attention and reinforcing the gains in areas that have responded well. Most clients complete 4–8 sessions for their primary areas of concern.

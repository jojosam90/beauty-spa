About the Treatment

Permanent Fat Reduction — Targeted, Not General

Most people have at least one area of stubborn fat that refuses to respond to even the most disciplined diet and exercise regime. These localised fat deposits — the belly pouch, love handles, inner thighs, bra fat, or double chin — are not a failure of willpower. They are areas where the body preferentially stores fat, and where exercise increases muscle beneath the fat rather than removing the fat layer above it.

EstheClinic's stubborn fat removal treatments offer a scientifically validated, non-surgical solution for precisely these deposits. Our protocol combines three complementary technologies to maximise fat reduction and body contouring results.

Cryolipolysis (Fat Freezing) is the most clinically documented non-surgical fat reduction technology in the world. It works by cooling subcutaneous fat cells to -9°C — a temperature that triggers apoptosis (controlled cell death) in fat cells while leaving the skin and surrounding structures completely unaffected. The treated fat cells are then gradually cleared from the body through the lymphatic system over 6–12 weeks. Ultrasound Cavitation uses focused low-frequency ultrasound to disrupt fat cell membranes, releasing their contents for lymphatic clearance. RF Body Contouring complements both by tightening the overlying skin as fat volume reduces — preventing looseness in the treatment area.

The Process

How It Works

A systematic, science-backed approach to eliminating stubborn fat deposits permanently.

  1. 01

    Body Composition Analysis

    Your consultation begins with a body composition analysis — we assess your body fat percentage, distribution pattern, and the specific fat pockets you want to address. We take baseline measurements and photographs of each target area. This data allows us to track your results objectively and ensures the treatment is delivering the outcomes we promised. We also discuss your overall health, weight history, and lifestyle to ensure you're an appropriate candidate for fat reduction treatment.

  2. 02

    Target Area Identification

    We work with you to identify and prioritise the areas of most concern. The treatment areas are carefully mapped and measured — this baseline data is used to select the appropriate applicator size for cryolipolysis and the optimal settings for cavitation and RF. Not all fat deposits are suitable for every technology — our specialist will recommend the most effective approach for each specific area.

  3. 03

    Fat Reduction Sessions

    Cryolipolysis sessions take 35–60 minutes per applicator. A gel pad is placed over the skin, then the applicator draws the tissue in with gentle suction and cools it to the therapeutic temperature. You'll feel intense cold for the first few minutes, which then numbs and becomes comfortable. Cavitation sessions follow — the ultrasound handpiece is applied with gel across the target area for 20–30 minutes. RF contouring is applied last to warm and tighten the skin over the treated zone. Multiple areas can be treated in a single extended session.

  4. 04

    Contouring & Monitoring

    The full results of cryolipolysis develop over 6–12 weeks as the eliminated fat cells are cleared through the lymphatic system. We schedule progress reviews at 6 and 12 weeks post-treatment to measure fat reduction against your baseline. Additional sessions can be scheduled for any areas requiring more reduction. Many clients choose to treat multiple areas across a planned programme — our team will build a comprehensive contouring schedule aligned with your goals.

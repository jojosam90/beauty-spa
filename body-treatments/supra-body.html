The Process

How the Supra Body Programme Works

A structured, evolving 12-session journey — designed for total body transformation.

  1. 01

    Full Body Analysis

    Your Supra Body journey begins with the most comprehensive body assessment we offer. Our senior specialist conducts a full body composition analysis, skin laxity assessment, cellulite grading across all affected zones, fat deposit mapping, and skin tone evaluation. Advanced body imaging is used to document every area of concern. We take detailed measurements, photographs, and build a complete picture of your body's current state — this is the baseline against which your transformation will be measured.

  2. 02

    Personalised Programme Design

    Based on your full body analysis, we design your bespoke 12-session Supra Body Programme. This involves selecting the specific technologies for each session, determining the treatment area prioritisation, and sequencing the sessions to ensure that each technology is applied at the optimal point in the programme. For example, skin tightening sessions are sequenced after fat reduction begins, as the reduction in fat volume makes skin tightening simultaneously more effective and more necessary. Your programme schedule is presented in full at the design consultation.

  3. 03

    Multi-Technology Sessions (12 Total)

    Your 12 sessions are typically structured over 3–4 months — roughly one session per week or every 10 days. Each session is 60–90 minutes and combines multiple technologies sequenced for maximum synergy. Early sessions typically emphasise cellulite treatment and lymphatic stimulation (to enhance circulation before fat reduction begins). Mid-programme sessions introduce fat reduction and skin tightening. Later sessions refine and consolidate the results, shifting to brightening, skin quality, and collagen refinement. Each session is logged, and the protocol is refined at each of the four built-in progress review points.

  4. 04

    Progress Reviews & Adjustments

    The Supra Body Programme includes formal progress reviews at sessions 4, 8, and 12 — with detailed photography, measurement comparisons, and a specialist discussion of your results and the remaining programme. This structured review process ensures the programme is delivering results in line with your goals, and allows us to reallocate treatment focus if any particular area is progressing faster or slower than anticipated. At the final review (session 12), we document your full transformation and design your post-programme maintenance plan.

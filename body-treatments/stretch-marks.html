About the Treatment

Visible Improvement for Stretch Marks — Old and New

Stretch marks (striae) are one of the most common dermatological concerns affecting both women and men — and one of the most resistant to topical treatments. They form when the skin is stretched rapidly beyond its elastic capacity, causing micro-tears in the dermis. These tears — which appear as red or purple lines (striae rubra) in their early stages and white or silver atrophic scars (striae alba) over time — cannot be reversed by creams or oils, which simply cannot penetrate to the depth of the damage.

EstheClinic's stretch mark treatment protocol uses three technologies that work at the level of the dermis — where the damage actually lies. Fractional CO2 Laser creates a grid of micro-ablative channels within the stretch mark, triggering a deep wound-healing response that produces fresh, organised collagen to fill and smooth the scar tissue. RF Microneedling combines the mechanical stimulation of microneedling with radiofrequency energy delivered at the precise depth of the dermis — powerfully stimulating collagen and elastin production in the exact location of the stretch mark. Collagen Induction Therapy (CIT) adds a third layer of collagen stimulation using growth factor serums driven into the skin through micro-channels.

The combination of these three approaches produces meaningful, visible improvements in texture, colour, and depth — applicable to stretch marks at any stage, including years-old silvery striae alba that many treatments cannot address.

Treatment Benefits

Progressive fading and smoothing — with results that improve long after treatment ends.

Fades Stretch Marks

The discolouration of stretch marks — red, purple, or silver — responds significantly to our laser and RF treatments. The colour differential between stretch marks and surrounding skin diminishes progressively with each session, making marks far less visible.

Improves Skin Texture

Stretch marks have a distinct depressed, grooved texture compared to surrounding skin. As new collagen fills the scar tissue in response to treatment, the surface of the stretch mark gradually rises and levels with surrounding skin — improving the tactile and visual texture dramatically.

✓

Stimulates Collagen Production

All three of our technologies trigger collagen synthesis at the dermal level — the layer where stretch marks exist. This fresh, organised collagen remodels the scar tissue, rebuilding the structural integrity of the skin from within.

✓

Restores Smoother Skin

After a course of treatment, treated areas have a notably smoother overall skin surface — the boundaries between stretch marks and healthy skin become progressively less defined until, in many cases, they are barely discernible.

✓

Effective on Old Marks

Many treatments only address newly formed (red) stretch marks. Our Fractional CO2 and RF Microneedling protocol is clinically effective on years-old silver and white stretch marks — a significant differentiator from most surface-level treatments.

Collagen synthesis triggered by each session continues for 3–6 months after treatment. This means your results improve progressively long after your last session — clients often see their best improvements at the 3–6 month review.

The Process

How It Works

A targeted, collagen-driven approach to fading and smoothing stretch marks from the inside out.

  1. 01

    Stretch Mark Assessment

    Your consultation begins with a detailed assessment of your stretch marks — our specialist evaluates their age (striae rubra vs alba), distribution, depth, and the skin type and tone of the surrounding area. We document the affected areas with baseline photographs and discuss the realistic level of improvement that is achievable for your specific marks. The cause of the stretch marks (pregnancy, weight change, growth, or genetic predisposition) is also reviewed as this affects the treatment protocol.

  2. 02

    Collagen Induction Protocol

    We design a bespoke collagen induction protocol for your specific stretch marks — selecting the most appropriate combination of Fractional CO2, RF Microneedling, and CIT, and planning the intensity and depth of each treatment. Newer (red/purple) stretch marks may respond well with RF Microneedling alone. Older, deeper, and wider (silver/white) marks typically require the full combination protocol including Fractional CO2 for maximum impact.

  3. 03

    Laser & RF Sessions

    Sessions take 45–75 minutes depending on the area being treated. Topical anaesthetic cream is applied 30–45 minutes before treatment to maximise comfort. The Fractional CO2 or RF Microneedling handpiece is applied methodically across the stretch mark area. You'll feel warmth and a degree of pressure — the topical anaesthetic makes the procedure very manageable. Following the active treatment, a growth-factor serum is applied and driven into the micro-channels created for maximum collagen stimulation. Sessions are spaced 4–6 weeks apart to allow the skin to recover and respond.

  4. 04

    Skin Regeneration Phase

    After each session, the skin enters an active regeneration phase. The treated area may appear mildly pink or red for 24–48 hours (similar to moderate sunburn), and there may be some peeling in the days following Fractional CO2 treatment. We provide a comprehensive post-treatment care kit with a repairing serum, calming moisturiser, and high-SPF protection for treated areas. Progress is photographed and reviewed at 4 weeks and 3 months after each treatment to track improvements objectively.

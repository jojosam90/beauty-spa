About the Treatment

The Science Behind Cellulite — and Why Exercise Alone Isn't Enough

Cellulite affects approximately 85–90% of women — regardless of body weight, fitness level, or lifestyle. It is not a sign of being overweight, and it cannot be eliminated by diet or exercise alone. Understanding why is key to understanding why our treatment works.

Cellulite forms when subcutaneous fat cells push upward through weakened collagen fibres (septa) that connect the skin to the underlying muscle. The result is the characteristic dimpled appearance on the thighs, buttocks, abdomen, and arms. Because the structural issue lies within the fibrous connective tissue — not simply in the fat itself — surface-only approaches (creams, massage, diet) produce only temporary and minimal improvement.

EstheClinic's cellulite protocol uses three technologies that address the problem structurally. Acoustic Wave Therapy (AWT) transmits mechanical pressure waves through the tissue — breaking down fibrous septa, stimulating collagen production, and improving the skin's structural integrity. Vacuum-assisted RF combines radiofrequency heat with suction — heating the fat layer, tightening overlying skin, and stimulating lymphatic drainage simultaneously. Manual and mechanical Lymphatic Drainage reduces fluid retention and toxic accumulation in the tissue — a key factor in the severity of cellulite appearance.

The Process

How It Works

A comprehensive, science-backed approach to reducing cellulite where it starts.

  1. 01

    Body Assessment

    Your consultation begins with a thorough body assessment — our specialist evaluates the grade of your cellulite (I–III), the affected areas, skin firmness, and circulatory health. We take baseline photographs and measurements to track your progress objectively. We also discuss your lifestyle, diet, exercise habits, and any previous treatments, so we can build a protocol that works with — and enhances — your existing routine.

  2. 02

    Mapping Cellulite Areas

    Before your first treatment session, we map the specific zones of concern — identifying the most affected areas within each body region and planning the treatment intensity and sequence for each zone. This mapping is reviewed and updated at each visit as areas improve at different rates, allowing us to focus effort where it is most needed at each stage of the programme.

  3. 03

    Combination Technology Treatment

    Each session combines Acoustic Wave Therapy and Vacuum-assisted RF in sequence. AWT is applied first — the handpiece delivers rhythmic pressure waves across the treatment area (the sensation is similar to a deep tissue massage). Vacuum RF follows, warming the tissue with radiofrequency while the suction mechanism lifts and manipulates the skin for optimal penetration. Each session takes 45–75 minutes depending on the number of areas treated.

  4. 04

    Lymphatic Drainage & Maintenance

    After each active treatment session, a lymphatic drainage massage is performed to support the clearance of broken-down fat cell debris and stimulate healthy fluid circulation. Between sessions, we provide specific guidance on hydration, nutrition, and movement to enhance your results. After completing your course (typically 8–12 sessions), we schedule maintenance sessions and a long-term lifestyle plan to protect your investment.

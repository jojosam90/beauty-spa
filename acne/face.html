About the Treatment

Comprehensive Acne Care — From Active Breakouts to Clear Skin

Acne is one of the most emotionally impactful skin conditions a person can experience — affecting self-esteem, social confidence, and daily wellbeing at any age. Despite its prevalence, effective treatment requires a multi-faceted approach that addresses the root causes: bacterial overgrowth, excess sebum, inflammation, and clogged pores. Over-the-counter products and basic facials rarely achieve lasting results for moderate to severe acne.

EstheClinic's Face Acne Treatment programme deploys a powerful combination of clinical technologies designed to work at every level. LED Blue Light Therapy targets and kills P. acnes bacteria within follicles without any skin damage. IPL Acne Treatment reduces sebaceous gland activity, shrinks inflamed lesions, and destroys bacteria with multi-wavelength light. Salicylic Acid Treatments deeply exfoliate pore linings and dissolve comedones. Fractional Laser resurfacing addresses post-acne scarring and textural damage, while RF Pore Tightening minimises enlarged pores that trap debris and perpetuate breakouts.

This is a treatment programme — not a one-session fix. But the results are genuine, progressive, and lasting.

  • Blackheads and whiteheads (comedones)
  • Inflammatory papules and pustules
  • Cystic and nodular acne
  • Hormonal acne
  • Post-acne marks (PIH) and enlarged pores

Treatment Benefits

Benefits of Face Acne Treatment

A comprehensive approach that delivers clear skin today and prevents breakouts tomorrow.

✓

Clears Active Breakouts

Blue Light and IPL treatments directly target active acne bacteria, reducing existing lesions and calming inflamed breakouts progressively over each session. Clients typically notice a significant reduction in active pimples within the first 2–3 sessions.

✓

Reduces Future Acne

By targeting the bacteria and sebaceous gland activity that drive acne formation — rather than just managing existing breakouts — our treatments reduce the frequency and severity of future breakouts over time. The longer you continue the programme, the fewer breakouts you experience.

✓

Minimises Enlarged Pores

RF Pore Tightening uses radiofrequency energy to stimulate collagen around pore walls — physically shrinking enlarged pores and making them less prone to clogging. Smaller pores mean less comedone formation and a visibly smoother skin texture.

✓

Fades Post-Acne Marks

Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation (PIH) — the dark marks left behind after pimples heal — is treated with IPL and targeted brightening support. Fractional Laser addresses textural scarring left by more severe breakouts. Together, they restore an even, smooth skin surface.

✓

Balanced Oil Production

IPL acne treatments selectively suppress overactive sebaceous gland activity — reducing the excess oil production that feeds acne bacteria and contributes to clogged pores. Many clients report their skin feels and looks less oily within weeks of starting treatment.

✓

Clear, Confident Skin

The ultimate benefit is a life lived without the constant anxiety of new breakouts. Our clients consistently report not just clearer skin, but a profound improvement in confidence, reduced anxiety about their appearance, and a renewed willingness to engage socially without self-consciousness.

The Process

How It Works

A systematic, expert-guided approach from acne diagnosis to clear, confident skin.

  1. 01

    Acne Type Assessment

    Every acne case is different. Your specialist begins with a thorough assessment — identifying the types and grades of acne present, evaluating skin oil levels and pore condition, documenting the distribution and severity of post-acne marks and scarring, and reviewing potential contributing factors including hormonal health, diet, stress, and skincare habits. This assessment forms the foundation of your entire treatment plan.

  2. 02

    Personalised Acne Protocol

    Based on your assessment, your specialist designs a fully personalised multi-technology protocol — selecting the right combination of Blue Light, IPL, Salicylic treatments, and potentially Fractional Laser and RF, matched to your acne type and severity. We set honest, realistic expectations about timelines and outcomes, and provide a complete at-home skincare regimen recommendation to support your in-clinic treatments between sessions.

  3. 03

    Treatment Sessions (Weekly / Bi-Weekly)

    During the active treatment phase, sessions are scheduled weekly or bi-weekly to maintain consistent therapeutic pressure on the acne-causing bacteria and inflammation cycle. Each session typically combines two or more technologies — for example, Blue Light therapy followed by Salicylic Acid treatment, or IPL followed by soothing LED. Sessions are 45–75 minutes. Most clients see meaningful improvement after 3–4 sessions and significant clearance after 8–12 sessions depending on acne severity.

  4. 04

    Clear Skin Maintenance

    Acne management is an ongoing process rather than a one-time cure. After achieving your target clearance, we transition to a maintenance programme — typically monthly sessions — to prevent relapse. We also review and refine your home skincare routine, discuss dietary and lifestyle optimisations, and provide guidance on managing hormonal acne triggers. The goal is genuinely clear skin sustained long-term, not just a temporary improvement.

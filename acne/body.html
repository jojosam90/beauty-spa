About the Treatment

Clearing Body Acne — Where Breakouts Are Hardest to Treat

Body acne — particularly back acne ("bacne"), chest acne, and shoulder acne — is extremely common but often ignored or poorly managed. The skin on the back and chest has a higher density of sebaceous glands than most body areas, making it inherently prone to breakouts. Contributing factors include sweating, friction from clothing and sports gear, hormonal fluctuations, bacteria build-up, and post-workout skin care neglect.

The challenge with body acne is scale and access. The large surface area of the back makes consistent topical treatment difficult, and many over-the-counter products don't deliver active ingredients at the depth needed to affect follicular bacteria and sebum production in body skin (which is significantly thicker than facial skin).

EstheClinic's Body Acne Treatment uses clinical-grade technologies scaled for the body: IPL Body Acne Treatment covers large areas efficiently with broad-spectrum light that penetrates deep enough to affect body sebaceous glands. LED Therapy and Blue Light Panels deliver targeted antibacterial light across wide areas in a single session. Salicylic Acid Body Treatment provides deep chemical exfoliation to clear pore linings and break down existing comedones and inflammatory lesions.

The Process

How It Works

A targeted, systematic approach to clearing body acne — with ongoing prevention built in.

  1. 01

    Body Acne Assessment

    Your consultation begins with a thorough assessment of your body acne — evaluating the type, severity, and distribution of breakouts across all affected areas, assessing the degree of post-acne marking and scarring, and reviewing lifestyle factors that may be contributing to your breakouts (sports habits, clothing choices, showering routine, diet, hormones). Baseline photographs are taken for progress tracking. This assessment allows us to design a protocol precisely matched to your specific body acne pattern.

  2. 02

    Target Area Protocol

    Based on your assessment, we define the treatment areas and select the technologies best suited to your acne type and skin. For large areas like the full back, IPL Body Acne Treatment and Blue Light Panels provide the most efficient coverage. For localised or particularly dense acne zones, we may focus higher-intensity treatment. Your at-home body acne routine is also reviewed and optimised at this stage — an important part of treatment success that is often overlooked.

  3. 03

    Treatment Sessions

    Sessions are scheduled weekly or bi-weekly during the active treatment phase to maintain consistent therapeutic effect. Each session combines IPL or Blue Light Panel treatment with Salicylic Acid body treatment where appropriate. Sessions for the full back and chest take approximately 45–60 minutes. Most clients require 6–10 sessions for significant clearance, though some cases respond faster. Your specialist monitors progress at each visit and adjusts the protocol as your skin responds.

  4. 04

    Skin Maintenance & Prevention

    Once your target clearance is achieved, we transition to a maintenance and prevention phase — typically monthly sessions to sustain results and prevent relapse. Your specialist will provide a comprehensive body acne prevention guide covering shower habits, post-gym skincare, clothing fabrics, and a recommended body care routine with appropriate active ingredients. Many clients find that after completing their primary course, even occasional maintenance sessions are sufficient to keep body acne from returning.

About the Treatment

Flawlessly Smooth Facial Skin — Permanently

Unwanted facial hair on the upper lip, chin, sideburns, cheeks, forehead, or neck can be a significant source of self-consciousness. Daily grooming routines — threading, waxing, bleaching, or shaving — are time-consuming, costly, and often cause skin irritation, ingrown hairs, and post-inflammatory darkening.

EstheClinic's facial laser hair removal uses the Soprano ICE Platinum diode laser — the gold standard in permanent hair reduction. Its unique triple-wavelength technology (755 nm, 810 nm, 1064 nm) targets hair follicles at multiple depths simultaneously, ensuring complete and lasting results across all hair and skin types — including darker skin tones that many other lasers cannot safely treat.

Treatments are virtually pain-free thanks to the built-in ICE cooling system. Most clients describe the sensation as a warm, gentle gliding feeling — nothing like the discomfort of traditional laser systems. A full course of 6–8 sessions, spaced 4–6 weeks apart, delivers 90%+ permanent hair reduction.

Why Choose Laser

Benefits of Facial Laser Hair Removal

More than just convenience — laser hair removal transforms the health and appearance of your facial skin.

✓

Permanent Results

Unlike threading or waxing which remove hair temporarily, laser targets and destroys the follicle itself. After a full course, hair simply stops growing back.

✓

No More Threading or Bleaching

End your reliance on painful threading appointments and harsh bleaching chemicals. Your skin will thank you — no more redness, sensitivity, or chemical exposure.

✓

Eliminates Ingrown Hairs

Threading and waxing are notorious for causing ingrown hairs on the chin and jawline. Laser removes the follicle entirely, so ingrown hairs become a thing of the past.

✓

Even Skin Tone

Regular hair removal methods often cause PIH (post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation), leading to dark patches. Laser is gentle enough to improve rather than worsen skin tone over time.

✓

Safe for All Skin Types

The Soprano ICE Platinum's multi-wavelength system is clinically proven safe and effective for Fitzpatrick skin types I through VI — including darker Asian, Middle Eastern, and Afro skin tones.

✓

Completely Pain-Free

The ICE cooling system continuously chills the skin surface throughout treatment. No numbing cream required. Clients routinely describe it as one of the most comfortable aesthetic treatments they've experienced.

The Process

How It Works

A structured, expert-led approach from consultation to clear, smooth results.

  1. 01

    Free Consultation

    Your journey begins with a no-obligation consultation with one of our certified laser specialists. We discuss your hair removal goals, review your medical history, assess your skin and hair type, and answer every question you have. There is no pressure — just honest, professional guidance.

  2. 02

    Skin Assessment & Patch Test

    Before your first full session, we perform a thorough skin assessment using advanced imaging tools. A patch test is conducted on a small area to confirm your skin's response to the laser energy and calibrate the ideal settings for your unique skin and hair profile.

  3. 03

    Treatment Sessions

    Each facial hair removal session takes approximately 15–30 minutes depending on the areas treated. The Soprano ICE Platinum handpiece glides smoothly over the skin while emitting controlled bursts of laser energy. You will feel gentle warmth — nothing uncomfortable. Sessions are spaced 4–6 weeks apart to coincide with each hair growth cycle, ensuring all follicles are effectively targeted.

  4. 04

    Maintenance Check

    After completing your recommended course of 6–8 sessions, we schedule a review appointment to assess your results. Most clients achieve 90%+ permanent reduction. For the small number of resilient follicles that may occasionally produce fine hair, a simple top-up session once or twice a year maintains perfect results long-term.

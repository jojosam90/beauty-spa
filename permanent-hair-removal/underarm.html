The Process

How It Works

Fast, simple, and effective — here's what your underarm laser journey looks like.

  1. 01

    Free Consultation

    We begin with a quick, friendly consultation to understand your goals and confirm your suitability for treatment. Our specialist will assess your skin tone, hair type, and underarm skin condition — and explain exactly what to expect from the treatment course. No obligation to proceed, and no pushy sales.

  2. 02

    Skin Preparation & First Session

    You'll be asked to shave both underarms 24 hours before your first session. On the day of treatment, ensure the area is clean and free of deodorant or antiperspirant. Our therapist will clean the area, apply a thin layer of cooling gel, and begin the 15-minute laser treatment — it's that simple.

  3. 03

    Treatment Sessions

    Each underarm session takes approximately 15 minutes. The Soprano ICE Platinum handpiece glides across both underarms simultaneously delivering laser energy while maintaining a comfortable skin temperature. Sessions are scheduled every 4–6 weeks. After each session, you may notice the treated hairs falling out over the following 1–2 weeks — this is completely normal and a sign the treatment is working.

  4. 04

    What to Do Before Your Session

    Shave underarms 24 hours before your appointment. Avoid applying deodorant, perfume, or any product to the underarm area on the day of treatment. Avoid sunbeds and prolonged sun exposure 2 weeks before each session. Arrive with clean, dry underarms and enjoy your 15 minutes of treatment.

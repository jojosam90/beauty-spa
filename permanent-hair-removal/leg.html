The Process

How It Works

A straightforward journey from your first appointment to permanently smooth legs.

  1. 01

    Free Consultation

    We begin with a personalised consultation to determine which leg areas you want to treat — full leg, lower, upper, or knees specifically. Our specialist assesses your skin and hair type, discusses your lifestyle and goals, and recommends the ideal package for you. You'll leave with a clear picture of costs, timelines, and expected results — with no pressure to commit on the day.

  2. 02

    Skin Assessment & Patch Test

    Before your first full session, we perform a skin assessment and patch test to calibrate the laser settings precisely for your skin and hair type. This ensures your treatments are as effective and safe as possible from the very start — especially important for clients with darker skin tones who require specific wavelength adjustments.

  3. 03

    Treatment Sessions

    Each session is comfortable and efficient. The Soprano ICE Platinum's large treatment head moves smoothly across the leg, covering the full surface area systematically. A full leg session typically takes 45–60 minutes, while a half-leg session takes 25–35 minutes. You'll feel a gentle warmth with no sharp pain. Sessions are scheduled every 4–6 weeks to capture each wave of hair in its active growth phase.

  4. 04

    Maintenance Check

    After completing your recommended 6–10 sessions, we conduct a results review. Most clients achieve exceptional permanent reduction. For any occasional fine hairs that remain, a simple top-up session once or twice a year is all that's needed. We provide personalised aftercare guidance to protect and extend your results long-term.

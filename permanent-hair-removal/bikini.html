About the Treatment

Permanent Bikini Confidence — On Your Terms

The bikini area is one of the most requested and most impactful areas for permanent laser hair removal. Whether you're bothered by razor rash after every shave, the discomfort of regular waxing, or simply want the freedom of never having to think about it again — laser hair removal offers a lasting solution.

At EstheClinic, we offer four levels of bikini treatment to suit your personal preference. Our female therapists handle all bikini and Brazilian treatments in private, comfortable treatment rooms, ensuring complete discretion and professionalism at every visit.

The Soprano ICE Platinum diode laser is the ideal technology for the sensitive bikini area. Its precise targeting capability and ICE cooling system make even Brazilian treatments surprisingly comfortable. With 6–10 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart, most clients achieve permanent, carefree results.

The Process

How It Works

A discreet, professional process from your first conversation to permanent results.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    Your consultation is conducted privately with a female laser specialist. We discuss your preferred bikini style (line, high, full, or Brazilian), your skin and hair type, and any previous hair removal history. There is no judgment — just expert, compassionate guidance. We'll explain the full process and answer every question before you commit to anything.

  2. 02

    Patch Test & Skin Assessment

    Before your first full session, we conduct a patch test on the treatment area to confirm the ideal laser settings for your skin tone and hair type. This takes just a few minutes and ensures your first full treatment is as safe and effective as possible.

  3. 03

    Treatment Sessions

    Each bikini session typically takes 15–30 minutes depending on the area chosen. The Soprano ICE Platinum handpiece is precisely guided across the treatment zone. The cooling system keeps the sensitive skin comfortable throughout. You'll need to shave the area 24 hours before each session — we'll guide you on exactly how to prepare.

  4. 04

    Results Review & Aftercare

    After your recommended 6–10 sessions, we review your results together. The vast majority of clients achieve 90%+ permanent hair reduction. We provide a tailored aftercare plan and advise on any top-up sessions if needed to address any remaining fine hairs. Most clients need no further treatment at all.

Your Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Most clients achieve excellent permanent results in 6–10 sessions, spaced 4–6 weeks apart. Bikini line treatments tend to respond faster (6–8 sessions), while full Brazilian treatments targeting coarser, denser hair may take 8–10 sessions. Your specialist will give you a personalised estimate at your consultation.

  • Many clients are pleasantly surprised by how comfortable Brazilian laser treatments are with the Soprano ICE Platinum. The continuous cooling technology significantly reduces the sensation compared to older laser systems or traditional waxing. Most clients rate the discomfort as a 2–3 out of 10 — far more comfortable than a wax. Sensitive areas may feel slightly more intense, but no numbing cream is typically required.

  • Sessions are spaced 4–6 weeks apart. This interval is calibrated to the hair growth cycle — specifically targeting the anagen (active growth) phase when the laser is most effective at disabling follicles. Going too soon between sessions means targeting follicles that aren't in the ideal phase, reducing effectiveness. Our scheduling team will help you keep to the optimal timeline.

  • Shave the treatment area 24 hours before your appointment — do not wax, thread, or pluck, as these remove the hair root which the laser needs to target. Avoid sun exposure on the area for 2 weeks before treatment. Come to your appointment with clean, dry skin and no lotions or creams applied to the bikini area. Avoid tight clothing on the day of treatment for comfort afterwards.

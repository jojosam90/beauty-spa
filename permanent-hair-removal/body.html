About the Treatment

Smooth Skin Across Every Part of Your Body

Body hair — whether it's a dense back, a hairy chest, or stubbly arms — can affect your confidence at the beach, the gym, or simply in your everyday life. Traditional methods like shaving cause razor bumps and rapid regrowth, while waxing is painful, expensive, and only temporary.

EstheClinic's body laser hair removal uses the Soprano ICE Platinum diode laser — the same award-winning technology trusted by dermatologists worldwide. Its large treatment head covers extensive body areas quickly and efficiently, meaning a full back treatment takes less time than you might expect.

The triple-wavelength system (755 nm, 810 nm, 1064 nm) penetrates to the exact depth of each hair follicle, disabling it at the root for permanent results. The ICE cooling handpiece keeps skin comfortable throughout. Most body areas require 6–10 sessions, spaced 4–6 weeks apart, for complete and lasting results.

The Process

How It Works

A clear, structured path from your first visit to permanent results.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    Your first visit is a relaxed, in-depth consultation with our laser specialist. We discuss all the body areas you want to treat, review your skin and hair type, medical history, and any previous hair removal methods. You'll receive a personalised treatment recommendation and transparent pricing — with no obligation to commit.

  2. 02

    Body Assessment

    Before your first treatment, we perform a detailed assessment of each body area — mapping hair density, thickness, and your skin's Fitzpatrick classification. This allows us to configure the Soprano ICE Platinum to the ideal parameters for your body, maximising both safety and effectiveness from the very first session.

  3. 03

    Treatment Sessions

    Each session is relaxed and comfortable. You'll lie down while our therapist glides the cooling laser handpiece across the treatment area. Session duration varies by area — back and chest treatments take 30–45 minutes, while arms and shoulders take 20–30 minutes. Sessions are scheduled 4–6 weeks apart to ensure each wave of hair follicles is caught in its active growth phase.

  4. 04

    Review & Maintenance

    After your course of 6–10 sessions, we conduct a results review to assess the level of hair reduction achieved. Most clients see 90%+ permanent reduction. For any remaining fine hairs, a simple annual top-up session keeps results perfectly maintained. We also provide personalised aftercare guidance to help you get the most from your investment.

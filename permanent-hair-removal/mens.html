The Process

How It Works

A discreet, professional experience from your first visit to lasting results.

  1. 01

    Free Consultation

    We begin with a private consultation — no judgment, just practical information. Our specialist will discuss which areas you want to treat, assess your hair density and skin type, and give you an honest assessment of how many sessions you're likely to need. Men typically have coarser, denser hair than women, which we fully factor into our recommendations. You'll receive transparent pricing with no hidden costs.

  2. 02

    Skin Assessment & Configuration

    Male skin and hair is distinct from female — typically with higher sebum production, coarser follicles, and greater hair density. Our therapists configure the Soprano ICE Platinum specifically for male hair and skin parameters. A patch test is conducted on each treatment area to confirm the optimal settings before beginning the full course.

  3. 03

    Treatment Sessions

    Sessions take place in a private treatment room. You'll be given a robe and asked to expose only the specific treatment area. The laser handpiece is guided methodically across the area — back sessions take 40–55 minutes, chest 20–30 minutes, shoulders 20–25 minutes. The ICE cooling makes the experience comfortable even for coarse male hair. Sessions are spaced 4–6 weeks apart.

  4. 04

    Review & Long-Term Maintenance

    After completing your course (6–12 sessions depending on area and hair density), we conduct a thorough results review. Because testosterone continues to be produced, men may occasionally develop new fine hairs over many years — though not a regrowth of treated follicles. We recommend an annual check-in, and a simple top-up session if any new growth needs addressing.

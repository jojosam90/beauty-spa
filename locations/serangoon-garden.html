Services Available at Serangoon Garden

Our Serangoon Garden clinic offers the full EstheClinic treatment menu, with particular expertise in HIFU and body contouring.

✨

Permanent Hair Removal

Full body laser hair removal using Soprano ICE Platinum. Face, underarms, legs, bikini, back, arms, and more. Pain-free. All skin tones.

✨

HIFU Face & Body Lifting

One of the first EstheClinic locations to introduce HIFU. Non-surgical face lifting, neck tightening, and brow lifting with medically proven results.

✨

Body Treatments

Cellulite removal, stubborn fat reduction, body contouring, sagging skin tightening, stretch mark treatment, and our signature Supra Body programme.

✨

Skin Brightening

Face and body brightening, Intensive Brightening Programme, IPL Photorejuvenation, Glutathione Infusion, and Vitamin C Infusion.

✨

Acne Treatments

Face and body acne treatment, Blue Light therapy, IPL Acne, Fractional Laser for scars, RF Pore Tightening, and post-acne mark fading.

✨

Supra Body Programme

Our flagship full-body transformation programme — first launched at Serangoon Garden in 2019. Combines body contouring, skin tightening, and brightening in one comprehensive package.

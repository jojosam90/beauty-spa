About the Programme

When Stubborn Pigmentation Needs More Than Standard Treatment

Not all pigmentation is created equal. Melasma, deep dermal hyperpigmentation, and years of cumulative sun damage are conditions that standard brightening facials and basic laser sessions simply cannot adequately address. These stubborn pigmentation issues are persistent, complex, and often require a sustained, multi-technology medical approach to produce meaningful results.

EstheClinic's Intensive Brightening Programme is a premium, comprehensive course designed specifically for these challenging cases. This is not a one-size-fits-all programme. Each client's protocol is individually designed following an in-depth skin analysis — factoring in pigmentation type, depth, skin tone, lifestyle triggers, and hormonal factors. The result is the most thorough, medically rigorous brightening approach available in Singapore.

The Process

How It Works

A rigorous, medically supervised process — from deep-dive analysis to long-term luminosity maintenance.

  1. 01

    In-Depth Skin Analysis & Photography

    The programme begins with the most comprehensive skin assessment we offer. Using advanced imaging — including UV photography to reveal sub-surface pigmentation invisible to the naked eye — we map all pigmentation deposits, assess their depth and type, evaluate your skin tone and sensitivity, and establish a detailed photographic baseline. We also review your hormonal health, sun habits, and previous treatment history to inform the programme design.

  2. 02

    Custom Intensive Programme Design

    Your specialist designs a fully individualised 10–15 session protocol selecting the precise combination of Pico Laser, Q-Switch Nd:YAG, IPL, and Mesotherapy suited to your pigmentation profile and skin type. Session sequencing is carefully planned — some technologies work most effectively in a specific order. We also prescribe oral brightening supplement support and topical brightening agents to use between sessions, maximising results throughout the programme.

  3. 03

    Multi-Technology Sessions

    Each treatment session deploys a combination of technologies as defined by your programme plan. Pico Laser and Q-Switch Nd:YAG target deep dermal pigmentation; IPL Photorejuvenation addresses surface and mid-level melanin clusters; Mesotherapy infuses brightening actives directly into the dermis where they work most effectively. Sessions are 60–90 minutes and are scheduled at optimal intervals — typically 2–4 weeks apart — to allow controlled skin healing and response between each treatment.

  4. 04

    Progress Tracking

    At every third session, we conduct a formal progress review — comparing standardised photographs, assessing pigmentation reduction, and adjusting the remaining sessions as needed. This adaptive approach ensures that if certain areas are responding faster than others, we can redirect treatment intensity to maximise efficiency. You will see your before/after images at each review — a genuinely motivating part of the process for most clients.

  5. 05

    Maintenance Phase

    After completing your primary Intensive Programme, we transition you to a structured maintenance phase. This typically involves quarterly maintenance sessions, a medical-grade at-home brightening skincare routine, ongoing oral supplement support, and comprehensive sun protection guidance. We schedule annual reviews to monitor pigmentation stability and intervene early if recurrence is detected — ensuring your results remain as close to programme-end quality as possible long-term.

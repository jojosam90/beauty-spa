About the Treatment

From Dull to Dazzling — Your Path to Radiant Skin

Singapore's tropical climate, pollution, and cumulative sun exposure take a toll on your complexion over time. Melanin overproduction leads to dark spots, uneven skin tone, and a general dullness that makes skin look tired and aged — even when you feel your best. Standard skincare products rarely penetrate deep enough to make a meaningful difference.

EstheClinic's Face Brightening programme combines the most effective clinical technologies — IPL Photorejuvenation, Laser Toning, Vitamin C Infusion, and Glutathione-based brightening therapy — to address pigmentation at its source. IPL and laser energy selectively break down melanin deposits without damaging surrounding tissue, while infusion therapies flood the skin with antioxidants and brightening actives that suppress future melanin formation.

The result is skin that doesn't just look brighter momentarily — it genuinely becomes more luminous and even-toned over the course of your personalised treatment plan. Most clients report a visible difference after the very first session.

  • Dull and lacklustre complexion
  • Uneven skin tone and patchy pigmentation
  • Dark spots and age spots
  • Sun damage and freckles
  • Mild to moderate hyperpigmentation

The Process

How It Works

A structured, expert-designed approach from your first consultation to your final radiant result.

  1. 01

    Skin Tone Assessment

    Your journey begins with a comprehensive skin analysis. Using advanced imaging, we evaluate your Fitzpatrick skin type, identify all areas of pigmentation and uneven tone, assess the depth of pigmentation deposits, and document your baseline complexion for progress tracking. This thorough assessment ensures we design a brightening protocol perfectly calibrated to your unique skin.

  2. 02

    Brightening Protocol Design

    Based on your assessment, our specialist designs a fully personalised brightening protocol — selecting the ideal combination of IPL Photorejuvenation, Laser Toning, and infusion therapies most suited to your pigmentation type and skin tone. We map out the number and frequency of sessions and set realistic, honest outcome expectations before any treatment begins.

  3. 03

    Laser & Infusion Sessions

    Each treatment session combines energy-based technology (IPL or Laser Toning) with infusion therapy. The laser or IPL step targets existing pigmentation, while Vitamin C or Glutathione infusion floods the skin with brightening actives that inhibit future pigment formation. Sessions are comfortable and typically 30–60 minutes. Most clients require 4–8 sessions spaced 2–4 weeks apart depending on the degree of pigmentation.

  4. 04

    Glow Maintenance Program

    After completing your primary brightening course, we design a maintenance programme to protect and sustain your results. This includes periodic top-up sessions, a personalised at-home skincare routine featuring proven brightening actives, and sun protection guidance. Singapore's intense sun means ongoing UV protection is critical — our team provides practical, realistic advice for daily life.

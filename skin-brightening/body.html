About the Treatment

Bright, Even Skin Tone — From Head to Toe

Uneven body skin tone is an extremely common concern in Singapore's tropical climate. Friction, heat, hormonal changes, sun exposure, and post-inflammatory responses from waxing or shaving all contribute to darkening in key areas — particularly the underarms, inner thighs, elbows, and knees. For many people, this causes genuine self-consciousness around beachwear, sleeveless clothing, and swimwear.

EstheClinic's Body Brightening programme combines IPL Body Brightening, Exfoliating Enzyme Treatments, Glutathione Infusion, and Vitamin Mesotherapy into a comprehensive protocol that addresses body pigmentation at every level. IPL targets melanin deposits in the skin, enzyme treatments accelerate cellular renewal at the surface, and infusion therapies work systemically to inhibit melanin production and restore even tone from within.

Unlike topical lightening creams that offer minimal and temporary results, our clinical approach delivers genuine, lasting improvement — visible across all the areas that matter most to you.

  • Darkened underarms
  • Inner thigh discoloration
  • Dark elbows and knees
  • Sun-damaged arms and legs
  • Uneven body skin tone

The Process

How It Works

A thorough, personalised approach — from full body analysis to a maintenance plan that keeps your results.

  1. 01

    Full Body Skin Analysis

    Your consultation begins with a comprehensive full-body skin assessment. Our specialist evaluates your skin tone, identifies all areas of concern, assesses the degree and type of darkening in each zone, and photographs your baseline for objective progress tracking. We also review relevant lifestyle factors — sun habits, shaving or waxing history, and hormonal health — that may be contributing to your pigmentation.

  2. 02

    Problem Area Mapping

    Before treatment begins, we create a detailed map of all areas to be brightened — noting the severity of pigmentation in each zone and the sequence in which they will be prioritised. This allows us to allocate treatment intensity appropriately, focus more sessions on the darkest areas, and track improvement area by area as your programme progresses.

  3. 03

    IPL / Infusion Sessions

    Each session combines IPL Body Brightening or Enzyme Treatment with Glutathione Infusion or Vitamin Mesotherapy, depending on the areas being treated. IPL sessions target melanin deposits directly, while infusion therapies support systemic brightening and melanin suppression. Sessions typically take 45–90 minutes depending on the number of areas treated. Most clients complete a course of 6–10 sessions, spaced 2–4 weeks apart.

  4. 04

    Maintenance Plan

    After completing your primary programme, we create a tailored maintenance plan to preserve your brightening results. This includes periodic top-up sessions (typically quarterly), a recommended at-home body care routine with gentle brightening products, and practical advice on preventing re-darkening through sun protection and friction reduction. Your specialist remains available for ongoing guidance throughout your journey.
